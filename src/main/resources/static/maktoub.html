We Welcome Submissions

Maktoub is open to submissions from all students interested in Islamic and Middle East studies. We accept various forms of writing including:

  • Academic essays and research papers
  • Book reviews
  • Personal reflections and narratives
  • Opinion pieces and commentary
  • Creative writing (poetry, short stories)
  • Research summaries and abstracts

Submission Guidelines

  • Submissions should be related to Islamic and/or Middle East studies plus what guidelines you have
